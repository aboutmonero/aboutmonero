Bitcoin was created to solve the problems of centralized payment systems yet the main method of purchasing bitcoin is through trusted centralized excahnges. Bisq is one solution built on pure P2P infrastructure: desktop software, Tor, local wallets, and no central accounts. While the transfer of national currency requires traditional payment channels like banks and payment processors, Bisq is not dependent on any particular one. [1]
