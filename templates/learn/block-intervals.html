Block intervals aboutℳonero

The block interval is the average duration of time between confirming blocks in the blockchain. For the Monero blockchain, there was initially a one minute block interval then later changed to two minutes. If there is in increase in the network hashrate, then blocks are produced faster. That is why there is a difficulty adjustment. As the network hashrate increases, the difficulty of confirming blocks also increases to try to maintain a two minute block interval.
