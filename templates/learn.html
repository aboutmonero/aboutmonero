Introduction

  • What is ℳonero?: ℳonero is a currency that allows fast, inexpensive payments to and from anywhere in the world. There are no multi-day holding periods and no risk of fraud. Monero accounts and balances are stored on a decentralized database known as a blockchain. This prevents centralized agencies from mediation and arbitration.

  • Privacy-focused: ℳonero uses cryptography to shield sending and receiving addresses, as well as transacted amounts. Thus ℳonero transactions are confidential and untraceable. Every ℳonero transaction, by default, obfuscates sending and receiving addresses as well as transacted amounts. This always-on privacy means that every ℳonero user's activity enhances the privacy of all other users, unlike selectively transparent cryptocurrencies.

  • Sound Money: Currencies have historically possessed many different properties. These properties are described by the Federal Reserve as: durability, portability, divisibility, uniformity, limited supply, and acceptability. In ancienct times, animals were used to act as a medium of exchange. Today, government-issued money largely plays the role of our main form of currency. Because of ℳonero's digital properties, it is a virtually perfect form of money.

  • Controlled Supply: The ℳonero network is governed by computer code established by the early founders. This code dictates the total supply of ℳonero to be created and at what rate. No central authority can intervene to create more new ℳonero than what is written in the computer code. Because the computer code is open to the public, the supply of ℳonero can always be known and verified. ℳonero possesses a disinflationary supply meaning that that supply is increasing at a decreasing rate. Current inflation rates are at about 3% and this will decrease to 0.87% by 2022.

  • Grassroots Development and Funding: ℳonero is a grassroots community attracting the world's best cryptocurrency researchers and engineering talent. Over 500 developers have contributed to the ℳonero project, including 30 core developers. Forums and chat channels are welcoming and active. ℳonero's Research Lab, Core Development Team and Community Developers are constantly pushing the frontier of what is possible with cryptocurrency privacy and security.
